What is this?

DesiMacros is a curated directory of nutrition information for popular Indian fast-food & restaurant chains — all from official sources. Every item includes calories, protein, carbs, and fat so you can make informed choices without hunting through PDFs.

Data Sources

Values come from official brand websites, FSSAI-compliant nutrition booklets, published menus, and manufacturer-provided nutritional information (FatSecret Branded Entries). We only include chains that have made their data publicly available.
