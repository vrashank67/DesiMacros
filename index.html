What is this?

DesiMacros is a curated directory of nutrition information for popular Indian fast-food & restaurant chains — all from official sources. Every item includes calories, protein, carbs, fat, and a serving note so you can make informed choices without hunting through PDFs.

Data Sources

All values come from official brand websites, FSSAI-compliant nutrition booklets, published menus, and manufacturer-provided nutritional information (FatSecret Branded Entries). We only include chains that have made their data publicly available.
